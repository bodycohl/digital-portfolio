Case Study - College Grads to New Cities

This case study follows the process of finding a solution to helping recent college graduates tackles the challenges of moving to a new place and having limited resources to meet people and become familiar with an area. My team wanted to provide our user group access to information about their new home and resources to find roommates, friends, restaurants and activities to allow them to settle into their new home as quickly as possible.
Commission Project - LEGS FIT

In August 2022, I was hired to create a website showcasing a friend's workout plan that she wanted to sell. I created mockups, designed the website from scratch using HTML, CSS, & JavaScript, and launched the website to a domain name. I managed to create a cohesive, functional webpage that allows users to learn about the instructor, view recipes for different meals, purchase the workout plan and download the files that become available after purchase.
